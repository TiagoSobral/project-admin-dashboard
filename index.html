Abilities

Heavenly Restriction

Kokichi was born without a right arm and both of his legs. His skin was so sensitive it couldn't be touched by natural light and the pores on his body always felt like they were being stabbed with needles.

Puppet Manipulation

Kokichi's technique allowed him to remotely control cursed corpse puppets over a wide range, encompassing the entirety of Japan due to his Heavenly Restriction.

Binding Vow

Kokichi Muta had a binding vow with Mahito's group, promising to give them information as a mole in exchange for a healthy body. One of the conditions Kokichi had was for Mahito's group to not hurt anyone from Kyoto Jujutsu High.

Ultimate Mechamaru

A mechanical cursed corpse that Kokichi originally used as his proxy to attend school and missions.

Miracle Cannon

Kokichi fires a blast of cursed energy from both of his puppet's hands.

Pigeon Viola

creates 5 orbs made of cursed energy and blasts from them when released. The blasts follow the target until they come in contact with something.
